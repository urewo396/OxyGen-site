The Opportunity

Why Graphite?

The world is shifting to Electric Vehicles, but the battery supply chain is dirty. An EV battery contains significantly more Graphite than Lithium.

Current graphite mining is highly carbon-intensive and vulnerable to supply shocks. OxyGen flips the script: we create Carbon-Negative Graphite by pulling the raw material directly from the air. It's not just a product; it's a solution to the battery bottleneck.
