Academic Partnership

Backed by rigorous research

OxyGen is an engineered solution developed with research partners at the Silesian University of Technology, bridging theoretical thermodynamics with real-world deployment.

01

Extract

Use next-generation direct air capture to pull CO2 from ambient air or emission-heavy locations and prepare it for conversion.

02

Convert

Thermal reaction cycles break molecular bonds so carbon can be separated while oxygen is returned to the environment.

03

Solidify

Stabilize the carbon into graphite: a profitable, durable material that matters to energy storage and advanced manufacturing.

The Opportunity

Why graphite, and why now?

The EV transition is accelerating, but the battery supply chain is still resource-intensive and exposed to major supply shocks. Each battery pack depends on far more graphite than lithium.

OxyGen flips the script by creating carbon-negative graphite from captured atmospheric CO2, pairing emissions removal with a material the market already needs.

  • Reduce reliance on carbon-intensive mined graphite.
  • Create value from captured carbon instead of treating it as waste.
  • Position climate hardware inside a fast-growing battery ecosystem.
50 kg Average graphite demand in a single EV battery
