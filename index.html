Backed by Rigorous Research

OxyGen is an engineered solution supported by research partners at the Silesian University of Technology. We bridge the gap between theoretical thermodynamics and real-world application.

01. Extract

Utilizing next-gen Direct Air Capture to pull CO2 straight from the sky. We target emissions at the source or from ambient air.

02. Convert

Thermal reactions break molecular bonds. Carbon is separated, and pure Oxygen is released back into the environment.

03. Solidify

The captured carbon is mineralized into graphite—a stable, profitable material essential for the battery industry.

The Opportunity

Why Graphite?

The world is shifting to Electric Vehicles, but the battery supply chain is dirty. An EV battery contains more Graphite than Lithium.

Current graphite mining is carbon-intensive. OxyGen flips the script: we create Carbon-Negative Graphite by pulling the raw material directly from the air. It's not just a product; it's a solution to the battery bottleneck.
